About Us

Company Overview

Our Company gives priority to Quality in all aspect. The company is willing to spend its time, energy and finance to secure quality service for honored customers. Our company makes every effort to become a Quality Coffee Exporter.

Our Values

At Tsedeke Bekele Coffee Exporter, quality is our highest priority. We dedicate our time, energy, and resources to ensuring exceptional service and premium coffee for our valued customers. Our commitment is to consistently provide QUALITY COFFEE and build long-term, trustworthy relationships with our partners.

Our Vision

Our vision is to promote Ethiopia's renowned Arabica coffee in the global market while creating sustainable and dependable business opportunities. We aim to contribute significantly to the growth of Ethiopia's economy by maintaining integrity, consistency, and excellence in every aspect of our operations.

Our Mission

Tsedeke Bekele Coffee Exporter is guided by a clear strategy focused on daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly objectives to continuously enhance performance and reliability. We strive to remain loyal, responsive, and dependable to our customers by understanding and adapting to their needs. Our efforts include expanding export capacity, staying informed about global market trends, and ensuring transparency and professionalism in all business dealings. Ultimately, our goal is to become a standard setter in Ethiopia's coffee export industry recognized for our professionalism, reliability, and commitment to quality.
